Nan Gyi Thoke

Nan gyi thoke is an a thoke salad dish in Burmese cuisine, made with thick round rice noodles mixed with specially prepared chicken curry and chili oil. The dish is garnished with toasted chickpea flour, sliced onions, chilis, crispy noodles, slices of hard-boiled egg, fish cakes, and zested with lime or lemon.










The ingredients

Receipe direction

Cooking the noodles

Boil your noodles in the boiling water and add a little bit of salt. Drain the noodles and wash with cold water. Then code with a drizzle of oil to prevent sticking each other.

Cooking the chicken

Dice chicken breasts into small cubes. Add cooking oil into a heated pan and throw chopped shallot and garlic. When you get a nice aroma, put in the diced chicken seasoned with salt and fish salt. Add turmeric and red chili powder. Then toss for a few times. Add chopped tomatoes (without seeds) to the pan, add a little bit of chicken stock, and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes.Now chicken curry sauce will be ready to serve with your noodles.

Serving Instructions

You can serve this noodle dish just like the way you serve your spaghetti – chicken curry sauce on top of the noodle. After the chicken sauce, add slices of boiled egg, yellow crispy fritters, roasted peanut powder, and some coriander leaves. Finally, drizzle lime and fish sauce. Enjoy!
